NAVIGATION GUIDE

How to Use This Website

A complete walkthrough of every feature in the navigation bar

Home

Clicking Home in the navbar scrolls you back to the very top of the page. Here you'll find the platform's headline, a brief introduction to what Cognifyz offers, and the main technology image. It's your starting point every time you visit.

Courses

The Courses link jumps you to the Explore Courses & Internships card area. Click the "Explore Courses & Internships" button there to dynamically load all five program cards — Frontend, Backend, Full Stack, AI, and Cloud — directly on the page without any page reload. Each card shows title, description, skill list, and internship info.

Internships

Clicking Internships takes you directly to the Registration Form section. This is where you formally apply for any internship at Cognifyz. Fill in your full name, email, phone number, create a secure password, and select your desired course to begin your journey.

Contact

The Contact link scrolls to the footer section which contains three contact boxes — General Queries, Internship Support, and Phone Support — along with social media links for LinkedIn, GitHub, and Instagram. Use these to reach the Cognifyz team for any questions or support you need.

Search Bar

The Search field in the top-right of the navbar lets you type keywords like "Frontend", "AI", "Cloud", or "MongoDB" to quickly find the course that matches your interest. Hit the Search button or press Enter to filter results. This is especially useful on mobile where scrolling through all content takes time.
